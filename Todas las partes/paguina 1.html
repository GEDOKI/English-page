Análisis

According to the Secretary of Traffic of Medellín, the Manrique the 45 sector is one of the areas of the city with the highest number of accidents, due to the recklessness of motorcyclists and pedestrians who use the exclusive lane of the Metroplús, breaking traffic rules. The driver education team took advantage of this sector to teach drivers and pedestrians the importance of using platforms, zebras, traffic lights, as well as safety elements when driving a motorcycle.

In 2015, 32,000 injured appeared in the city, and around 25,000 were motorcyclists, and of the 289 people who died, 60% were pedestrians and 40% motorcyclists. The campaign included a pedagogical intervention accompanied by the BTL de sombra. The objective is to show the public the figures for mortality and accidents on the roads.
