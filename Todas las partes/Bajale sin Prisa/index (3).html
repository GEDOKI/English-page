History


This campaign begins with a group of students from the multimedia production program at the National Learning Service (SENA) because we have to contribute to our educational area with a training project, with the purpose of contributing to a difficulty in commune 4 of Medellin. After several weeks of analysis of different aspects of the sector and its inhabitants, we found a problematic factor that affects this entire community (pedestrians, drivers, children) with the need to call for the safety of our inhabitants, we decided to create this campaign to contribute to this community.
